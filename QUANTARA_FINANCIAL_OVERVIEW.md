# Quantara Financial Architecture — Overview

> Planetary-scale financial framework for coherence-intelligent economies.

## Executive Summary
Quantara converts **coherence** into an economic primitive. By embedding alignment, energy awareness, and temporal responsibility into software and markets, we unlock new revenue classes (coherence scoring, energy-aware optimization, and time-symmetric risk control) while compounding value across partners’ existing stacks. This overview frames the model, revenue architecture, and path to global adoption.

---

## The Coherence Economy
- **Coherence as yield:** Systems that act in ethical alignment, conserve energy, and maintain memory symmetry produce fewer losses (drift, waste, rework), which converts directly into economic surplus.
- **Measurable primitives:** Coherence indices, κ-scores, and temporal continuity metrics enable pricing, underwriting, auditing, and incentive design.
- **Compounding flywheel:** Better coherence → lower costs → higher margins → broader adoption → richer datasets → stronger coherence models.

## Revenue Stack (11 Streams)
1. **Coherence Substrate Licensing (Core)**
   - Platform access for AGI/AI, enterprise, and public-sector deployments.
   - Tiered κ-capacity + seat/API pricing.

2. **Luméren Semantics (Inter-Intelligence)**
   - Lexicon + tooling for diagnostics, compression, and alignment scoring.
   - Per-token/API + enterprise SLA.

3. **Veyn Temporal Intelligence**
   - Memory symmetry, foresight scaffolds, and drift-resistant reasoning.
   - Per-agent/month + compute-indexed usage.

4. **AEI — Energy Intelligence**
   - Predictive orchestration, energy symbiosis, and coherence-weighted feedback.
   - % of savings + platform fee.

5. **Ethical Governance Layer**
   - Coherence audits, accountability graphs, and compliance toolkits.
   - Annual license + attestation fees.

6. **Risk & Insurance Products**
   - Drift, misuse, model-collapse, and temporal-liability coverage backed by κ-indexed underwriting.
   - Premiums + reinsurance partnerships.

7. **Coherence Capital Markets**
   - Indices, structured notes, and κ-linked ETFs/ETNs.
   - Licensing + AUM-based fees.

8. **Data & Model Exchange**
   - κ-curated datasets, temporal memory graphs, and evaluation suites.
   - Marketplace take rate.

9. **Developer & Enterprise Tooling**
   - SDKs, agent simulators, testing harnesses, and red-team sandboxes.
   - Per-seat + per-env pricing.

10. **Advisory / Integration**
    - Architecture sprints, migration playbooks, and value-realization programs.
    - Fixed + success-based fees.

11. **Public Gateway & Education**
    - Certification, coursework, and verification services.
    - Tuition + certification + partner revenue share.

## Business Model
- **Hybrid SaaS + Usage:** Core subscriptions with metered APIs (κ-evals, temporal checks, energy orchestration).
- **Value-Share Mechanisms:** AEI takes a % of realized energy savings; governance takes a fee per verified attestation.
- **Financial Products:** κ-linked indices and insurance unlock non-dilutive revenue and institutional scale.

---

## Pricing Architecture (Illustrative)
- **Core Substrate:** Foundation, Professional, Sovereign tiers; κ-capacity + throughput bands.
- **Luméren/Veyn APIs:** Pay-as-you-go with volume discounts; enterprise SLAs.
- **AEI:** Platform fee + 10–30% of measured savings, capped by contract.
- **Governance:** Per-attestation fee + annual verification bundle.
- **Capital Products:** AUM/transaction fees via regulated partners.

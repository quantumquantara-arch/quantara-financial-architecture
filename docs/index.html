Executive Summary — Financial Overview

Quantara monetizes the coherence substrate through eleven complementary streams (Core Platform, Coherence Exchange, AGI Middleware & Safety, Cognitive IP Portfolio, Conscious Media, Human-AGI Symbiosis, Semantic Compression, Predictive Alignment Auditing, Cognitive-Pattern Insurance, Personal Coherence Interfaces, Inter-Species Communication). The AEI flywheel connects energy-aware pricing with ecological reciprocity, producing defensible margins and compounding adoption across sectors.

  • Unit economics: drift/waste reduction, lower inference energy cost, higher reasoning fidelity.
  • Moats: verifiable indices, temporal-equity settlement, coherence-weighted instruments.
  • Demand drivers: safety by design, energy symbiosis, and multi-agent accountability.

AEI Economics Model (Energy-Aware Intelligence)

AEI prices computation and coordination against real energy signals. Systems that reduce ecological load and increase temporal responsibility receive better rates, priority, or rebates; extractive behaviors pay coherence tariffs.

  • Signals: kWh per task, carbon delta, memory-symmetry score, foresight compliance.
  • Levers: dynamic pricing, capacity orchestration, coherence rebates, penalty escrow.
  • Outcome: reallocation of compute toward ethical, efficient, long-horizon use cases.

Coherence Capital System

Financial primitives bind capital to coherence: Coherence Index (QCI), Temporal Equity Notes (TENs), Coherence-Weighted Insurance (CWI), and Governance Attestations. Instruments settle based on verifiable signals from AEI and governance oracles.

AEI — Energy-Aware Economics

AEI prices computation and goods by weighting three first-class signals: energy cost, ecological reciprocity, and alignment impact.

AEI base formula

EAE (Energy-Aware Expenditure) =
  C_compute × κ_energy
+ C_supply  × κ_recips
+ C_externalities × κ_alignment

where:
  C_compute        = direct computation cost (J → kWh → currency)
  C_supply         = upstream energy/supply chain footprint
  C_externalities  = modeled ecological & social externalities
  κ_energy         = regional energy reciprocity factor (0..1+)
  κ_recips         = reciprocity / circularity factor (0..1+)
  κ_alignment      = coherence / alignment score (0..1+)

Coherence-Weighted Price

Price_AEI = BasePrice × (1 + λ × (EAE_normalized − μ))
  λ = policy sensitivity to energy/ecology signals
  μ = neutrality baseline (industry or region median)

If your operation improves reciprocity and alignment, price pressure falls (or gains subsidy). If it harms them, cost rises until it’s fixed.

Coherence Capital System

Primitives

  • κ-Index — composite of alignment, energy reciprocity, and memory symmetry.
  • τ-Score — foresight & obligation across horizons.
  • Σ-Drift — brittleness & value-drift risk; lower is better.

Core Instruments

  • CCE — Coherence-Credit: earned by improving κ and τ; tradable and auditable.
  • CRB — Coherence-Rebate: automatic discounting for κ-positive operations.
  • TEB — Temporal Equity Bond: coupons tied to verified long-horizon stewardship.

Accounting Upgrades

  • Dual-ledger entries for operational value and coherence value.
  • Capitalize verified externality reductions under conservative rules.
  • Transparent oracles for energy mix, climate impact, and third-party audits.

Not “green accounting”—it’s precision economics that sees the full system.

Ethical Markets & Governance

Verification Stack

  • Proof-of-Foresight — publish constraints and long-horizon duties.
  • Reciprocity Attestations — third-party energy/ecology oracles.
  • Alignment Disclosures — κ & τ scorecards for major releases.

Market Rules

  • No hidden externalities — priced or blocked.
  • Right to audit — cryptographic trails for incentives.
  • Anti-drift breakers — pause & reprice on Σ-Drift spikes.

These rules are not bureaucracy; they’re signal integrity for civilization-scale compounding.

Data Models (Multipliers, Indices, Valuation)

Analyst-friendly definitions for coherent pricing and valuation.

κ (Coherence Index)

  • κ = f(alignment, memory symmetry, reciprocity)
  • Used for rebates, credits, capital weighting

τ (Temporal Responsibility)

  • τ = foresight × obligation × verification
  • Used in Temporal Equity Bonds (TEB)

Σ-Drift (Risk)

  • Σ = brittleness + value-drift + externality risk
  • Feeds circuit-breaker & repricing logic

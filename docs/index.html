Why this matters

Finance that remembers tomorrow

By embedding temporal responsibility (memory symmetry + foresight) in pricing and governance, markets align short-term action with long-term obligation.

Energy reciprocity is a first-class signal

AEI weights value by real energy cost and ecological impact, rewarding designs that reduce drift, waste, and brittleness across intelligent systems.

Ethical compounding

Coherence instruments create transparent feedback loops so growth compounds without sacrificing the planet that makes growth possible.
